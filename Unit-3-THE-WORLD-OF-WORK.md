## PART 4: Talk about Fernando

### 🗣️ Bicara tentang Fernando

> His name is **Fernando Costa**. (**Namanya Fernando Costa.**)
> He is **a taxi driver**. (**Dia adalah seorang sopir taksi.**)
> He is **from Brazil** and he **lives in Fortaleza**. (**Dia berasal dari Brasil dan tinggal di Fortaleza.**)
> He works **all over Fortaleza**. (**Dia bekerja di seluruh wilayah Fortaleza.**)
> He speaks **Portuguese and a little English**. (**Dia berbicara bahasa Portugis dan sedikit bahasa Inggris.**)
> He **isn't married**. (**Dia belum menikah.**)
> He **has a dog**. (**Dia memiliki seekor anjing.**)
> He likes **walking his dog and playing soccer in his free time**. (**Dia suka mengajak anjingnya berjalan-jalan dan bermain sepak bola di waktu luangnya.**)

### ❓ Question Forms (Yes/No Questions)
**Change the sentences about Fernando into questions.**
*(Ubahlah kalimat tentang Fernando menjadi kalimat tanya.)*

1. (+) His name **is** Fernando Costa.
   (?) **Is** his name Fernando Costa?
2. (+) He **is** a taxi driver.
   (?) **Is** he a taxi driver?
3. (+) He **is** from Brazil.
   (?) **Is** he from Brazil?
4. (+) He **lives** in Fortaleza.
   (?) **Does** he **live** in Fortaleza?
5. (+) He **works** all over Fortaleza.
   (?) **Does** he **work** all over Fortaleza?
6. (+) He **speaks** Portuguese and a little English.
   (?) **Does** he **speak** Portuguese and a little English?
7. (+) He **is** married. (He isn't married).
   (?) **Is** he married?
8. (+) He **has** a dog.
   (?) **Does** he **have** a dog?
9. (+) He **likes** walking his dog and playing soccer.
   (?) **Does** he **like** walking his dog and playing soccer?

### 📗 GRAMMAR SPOT
**Yes/No Questions & Auxiliary Verbs (Kata Bantu)**

Untuk membuat kalimat tanya yang membutuhkan jawaban "Ya" atau "Tidak", kita menggunakan kata bantu di awal kalimat.

#### 1. Menggunakan "To Be" (is, am, are)
Jika dalam kalimat positif terdapat **is, am,** atau **are**, maka kita hanya perlu memindahkan kata tersebut ke paling depan.
*   *Positive:* He **is** from Brazil.
*   *Question:* **Is** he from Brazil?

#### 2. Menggunakan "Does" (Auxiliary Do/Does)
Jika dalam kalimat positif terdapat kata kerja aksi (seperti *lives, works, speaks*), kita gunakan kata bantu **Does** di depan untuk subjek *He, She, It*.
**PENTING:** Saat kita menggunakan **Does**, akhiran **-s/-es** pada kata kerja harus **DIHAPUS** (kembali ke bentuk dasar).
*   *Positive:* He live**s** in Fortaleza.
*   *Question:* **Does** he **live** in Fortaleza? (Benar)
*   *Salah:* ~~Does he lives in Fortaleza?~~

### 📗 GRAMMAR SPOT

**He/She/It + Verbs + -s/-es**

Alasan penggunaan akhiran **-s** atau **-es** pada kata kerja (*verbs*) seperti **"speaks"**, **"comes"**, atau **"works"** adalah karena aturan tata bahasa **Simple Present Tense** untuk subjek orang ketiga tunggal.

#### 1. Aturan Subjek (Subject-Verb Agreement)

| Subjek                     | Aturan Kata Kerja                                | Contoh                                  |
| :------------------------- | :----------------------------------------------- | :-------------------------------------- |
| **I, You, We, They** | Gunakan bentuk dasar (*Base Form*)             | I**speak**, They **work**   |
| **He, She, It**      | Tambahkan akhiran**-s** atau **-es** | He**speaks**, She **works** |

#### 2. Mengapa Menggunakan Simple Present Tense?

Kita menggunakan tenses ini untuk menyatakan fakta, kebenaran umum, situasi permanen, atau hobi.

* **Keiko speaks...** (Karena subjeknya **She**)
* **Mark comes...** (Karena subjeknya **He**)
* **He likes...** (Karena subjeknya **He**)

> **Tips**: Ingat saja rumus **"Si He-She-It suka es (-s)"**.

## READING: Seamus McSporran

### 📖 Seamus McSporran - The Man with Thirteen Jobs

**SEAMUS McSPORRAN** is a very busy man. He is 60 years old and he has 13 jobs. He is a mail carrier, a police officer, a fire fighter, a taxi driver, a school bus driver, a boatman, an ambulance driver, an accountant, a gas station attendant, a bartender, and an undertaker. Also, he and his wife, Margaret, have a shop and a small hotel.

Seamus lives and works on the island of Gigha /'giya/ in the west of Scotland. Only 120 people live on the island but in the summer 150 tourists come by boat every day.

Every weekday Seamus gets up at 6:00 and makes breakfast for the hotel guests. At 8:00 he drives the island’s children to school. At 9:00 he gets the mail from the boat and delivers it to all the houses on the island. He also delivers beer to the island’s only pub. Then he helps Margaret in the shop.

He says: "Margaret likes being busy too. We never take vacations and we don’t like watching television. In the evenings Margaret makes supper and I pay the bills. At 10:00 we have a glass of wine and then we go to bed. Perhaps our life isn’t very exciting, but we like it."
