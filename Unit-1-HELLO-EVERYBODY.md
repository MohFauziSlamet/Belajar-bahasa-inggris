## PART 1: Read and Listen

### 📖 Baca dan Dengarkan

### Dialogue 1

**A:** Hello. My name's Paula. What's your name?
**B:** Rosa.
**A:** Where are you from, Rosa?
**B:** I'm from Chicago.

### 📗 GRAMMAR SPOT

| Contraction      | Full Form |
| :--------------- | :-------- |
| **name's** | name is   |
| **what's** | what is   |
| **I'm**    | I am      |

## PART 6: Ask and Answer

### 🗣️ Ajukan dan Jawab Pertanyaan

**Use he/his and she/her.**

### 📗 GRAMMAR SPOT

**Complete the chart with `am`, `is`, and `are`.**

| Subject | Verb          | Complement  |
| :------ | :------------ | :---------- |
| I       | **am**  |             |
| He      | **is**  |             |
| She     | **is**  |             |
| It      | **is**  | from Canada |
| We      | **are** |             |
| You     | **are** |             |
| They    | **are** |             |

### Examples

> 💭 **What's his name?** -> *Gordon.*
> 💭 **Where's he from?** -> *Canada.*

> 💭 **What's her name?** -> *Tomoko.*
> 💭 **Where's she from?** -> *Japan.*

## PART 9: Reading - Rafael and Yaling

### 📖 Baca dan Dengarkan

### Rafael Ramos

> My name's Rafael Ramos and I'm a doctor. I'm 30. I'm married and I have two children. I live in a house in Toluca in Mexico. I want to learn English for my job.

### Yaling Chen (Complete the text)

> **My** name's Yaling Chen and I'm a student. I **am** 19. I'm not married. I have one **sister** and two brothers. I **live** in an apartment in Taipei, Taiwan. I **want** to learn English because it's an international **language**.

**Missing words:** `live`, `language`, `want`, `My`, `sister`, `am`

## PART 12: Order the Conversation

### 🔢 Urutkan Percakapan

#### Conversation 1

1. **A:** Hello. Marty Freeman.
2. **B:** Hi, Marty. It's Jane. How are you?
3. **A:** Pretty good, thanks.
4. **B:** I'm fine, thanks. And you?

#### Conversation 2

1. **A:** Good-bye, Michael.
2. **B:** OK. See you then. Good-bye.
3. **A:** Is 7:00 OK with you, Bianca?
4. **B:** Yes. 7:00 is fine.

#### Conversation 3

1. **A:** Hello?
2. **B:** Hi, Flora! It's me, Leo. How are you?
3. **A:** Not bad, thanks. How are you?
4. **B:** Just fine. How are the children?
5. **A:** They're fine.
