## PART 1: Read and Listen

### 📖 Baca dan Dengarkan

### Dialogue 1

**A:** Hello. My name's Paula. What's your name? *(Halo. Nama saya Paula. Siapa nama kamu?)*
**B:** Rosa.
**A:** Where are you from, Rosa? *(Dari mana asalmu, Rosa?)*
**B:** I'm from Chicago. *(Saya berasal dari Chicago.)*

### 📗 GRAMMAR SPOT

| Contraction      | Full Form |
| :--------------- | :-------- |
| **name's** | name is   |
| **what's** | what is   |
| **I'm**    | I am      |

> [!NOTE]
> **Verb to be (am, is, are)** is used to introduce yourself or others. *(Kata kerja 'to be' digunakan untuk memperkenalkan diri sendiri atau orang lain.)*
> - **I am** (I'm) -> Used for yourself. *(Digunakan untuk dirimu sendiri.)*
> - **You are** (You're) -> Used for the person you are talking to. *(Digunakan untuk orang yang kamu ajak bicara.)*
> - **My name is** -> Used to say your name. *(Digunakan untuk menyebutkan namamu.)*

### 💡 Examples
- **Formal:** "Hello, I am Paula. Nice to meet you." *("Halo, saya Paula. Senang bertemu dengan Anda.")*
- **Informal:** "Hi, I'm Paula. What's up?" *("Hai, saya Paula. Ada apa? / Apa kabar?")*

> [!TIP]
> **Strategy:** Look at the words around the blank space. *(Strategi: Perhatikan kata-kata di sekitar bagian yang kosong.)*

| :---------------- | :-------- |
| **he's**    | he is     |
| **she's**   | she is    |
| **they're** | they are  |

> [!NOTE]
> **Subject Pronouns** help us talk about people without repeating names. *(Kata ganti subjek membantu kita membicarakan orang tanpa mengulang nama.)*
> - **He** -> Used for a man/boy (e.g., Rafael). *(Digunakan untuk seorang pria/laki-laki.)*

> - **They** -> Used for more than one person (e.g., Max and Lisa). *(Digunakan untuk lebih dari satu orang.)*

## PART 6: Ask and Answer

### 🗣️ Ajukan dan Jawab Pertanyaan

> [!TIP]
> **Grammar Note:** Questions often start with a "Wh-" word. *(Catatan Tata Bahasa: Pertanyaan sering dimulai dengan kata "Wh-".)*
> - **Structure:** Wh-word + be + subject? *(Struktur: Kata tanya Wh + to be + subjek?)*
> - **Example:** Where + is + he? *(Contoh: Di mana + (adalah) + dia?)*

**Use he/his and she/her.**

### 📗 GRAMMAR SPOT

**Complete the chart with `am`, `is`, and `are`.**

| Subject | Verb          | Complement  |
| :------ | :------------ | :---------- |
| I       | **am**  |             |
| He      | **is**  |             |
| She     | **is**  |             |
| It      | **is**  | from Canada |
| We      | **are** |             |
| You     | **are** |             |
| They    | **are** |             |

### Examples

> 💭 **What's his name?** -> *Gordon.* *(Siapa namanya? -> Gordon.)*
> 💭 **Where's he from?** -> *Canada.* *(Dari mana asalnya? -> Kanada.)*

> 💭 **What's her name?** -> *Tomoko.* *(Siapa namanya? -> Tomoko.)*
> 💭 **Where's she from?** -> *Japan.* *(Dari mana asalnya? -> Jepang.)*

### Practice with a Partner
- Point to a friend and ask: "What's his/her name?" *(Tunjuk seorang teman dan tanyakan: "Siapa namanya?")*
- Point to a photo and ask: "Where's he/she from?" *(Tunjuk sebuah foto dan tanyakan: "Dari mana asalnya?")*

## PART 8: Complete Sentences

### 📝 Lengkapi Kalimat

> [!NOTE]
> **Subject-Verb Agreement:** The verb must match the subject. *(Kesesuaian Subjek-Kata Kerja: Kata kerja harus cocok dengan subjeknya.)*
> - I **am**
> - He/She/It **is**
> - You/We/They **are**

**Complete the sentences with `am`, `is`, `are`, `her`, `his` or `your`.**

1. My name **is** Anna. *(Nama saya Anna.)*
2. Where **are** you from? *(Dari mana asalmu?)*
3. I **am** from Japan. *(Saya berasal dari Jepang.)*
4. "What's **your** name?" "My name's Tomoko." *("Siapa namamu?" "Nama saya Tomoko.")*
5. Max and Lisa **are** from Chicago. *(Max dan Lisa berasal dari Chicago.)*
6. This **is** my teacher. **His** name's Gordon. *(Ini guru saya. Namanya Gordon.)*
7. Where **is** he from? *(Dari mana asalnya?)*
8. This is my sister. **Her** name's Anna. *(Ini saudara perempuan saya. Namanya Anna.)*

### Yaling Chen (Complete the text)

> **My** name's Yaling Chen and I'm a student. I **am** 19. I'm not married. I have one **sister** and two brothers. I **live** in an apartment in Taipei, Taiwan. I **want** to learn English because it's an international **language**.

- **International:** Involving more than one country. *(Internasional: Melibatkan lebih dari satu negara.)*

## PART 12: Order the Conversation

### 🔢 Urutkan Percakapan

> [!TIP]
> **Conversation Flow:**
> 1. **Opening:** Hello / Hi. *(Pembukaan: Halo / Hai.)*
> 2. **Middle:** How are you? / Questions. *(Isi: Apa kabar? / Pertanyaan-pertanyaan.)*
> 3. **Closing:** Good-bye / See you. *(Penutup: Selamat tinggal / Sampai jumpa.)*

#### Conversation 1

1. **A:** Hello. Marty Freeman. *(Halo. Marty Freeman.)*
2. **B:** Hi, Marty. It's Jane. How are you? *(Hai, Marty. Ini Jane. Apa kabar?)*
3. **A:** Pretty good, thanks. *(Cukup baik, terima kasih.)*
4. **B:** I'm fine, thanks. And you? *(Saya baik, terima kasih. Dan kamu?)*

#### Conversation 2

1. **A:** Good-bye, Michael. *(Selamat tinggal, Michael.)*
2. **B:** OK. See you then. Good-bye. *(Oke. Sampai jumpa nanti. Selamat tinggal.)*
3. **A:** Is 7:00 OK with you, Bianca? *(Apakah jam 7:00 cocok untukmu, Bianca?)*
4. **B:** Yes. 7:00 is fine. *(Ya. Jam 7:00 tidak masalah.)*

#### Conversation 3

1. **A:** Hello? *(Halo?)*
2. **B:** Hi, Flora! It's me, Leo. How are you? *(Hai, Flora! Ini aku, Leo. Apa kabar?)*
3. **A:** Not bad, thanks. How are you? *(Lumayan, terima kasih. Apa kabar?)*
4. **B:** Just fine. How are the children? *(Baik-baik saja. Bagaimana kabar anak-anak?)*
5. **A:** They're fine. *(Mereka baik-baik saja.)*
