## PART 2: Grammar Spot - Yes/No Questions
### вң… Pertanyaan Ya/Tidak

### Forming Yes/No Questions with "To Be"

**Question Pattern:**
```
Is/Are + Subject + ...?
```

**Examples:**

| Question | Answer | Translation |
|----------|--------|-------------|
| Is she English? | No, she isn't. | Apakah dia orang Inggris? |
| Is she French? | No, she isn't. | Apakah dia orang Prancis? |
| Is she American? | Yes, she is. | Apakah dia orang Amerika? |

### Short Answers (Jawaban Singkat)

#### вң… Positive:
- **Yes, she is.** *(Ya, dia adalah)*
- **Yes, it is.** *(Ya, itu adalah)*
- **Yes, I am.** *(Ya, saya adalah)*

#### вқҢ Negative:
- **No, she isn't.** *(Tidak, dia bukan)*
- **No, it isn't.** *(Tidak, itu bukan)*
- **No, I'm not.** *(Tidak, saya bukan)*

### Negative Forms (Bentuk Negatif)

| Full Form | Contracted Form | Translation |
|-----------|-----------------|-------------|
| I am not | I'm not | Saya bukan |
| You are not | You aren't | Kamu bukan |
| He is not | He isn't | Dia (laki-laki) bukan |
| She is not | She isn't | Dia (perempuan) bukan |
| It is not | It isn't | Itu bukan |
| We are not | We aren't | Kami bukan |
| They are not | They aren't | Mereka bukan |

> вҡ пёҸ **NOTE:** "I amn't" is **INCORRECT!** Always use **"I'm not"**

### Grammar Spot: Possessive 'S (Kepemilikan 'S)

#### Two uses of **'s**:

#### 1пёҸвғЈ Contraction (She's = She is):
```
She's a teacher.     вҶ’ Dia adalah seorang guru.
He's from Japan.     вҶ’ Dia dari Jepang.
```

#### 2пёҸвғЈ Possession (Kepemilikan):
```

Brenda's job = her job          вҶ’ Pekerjaan Brenda = pekerjaannya
```

#### Examples:
**Q:** Who's Brenda? *(Siapa Brenda?)*
**A:** She's Patrick's wife. *(Dia adalah istri Patrick.)*

**Q:** What's her name? *(Siapa namanya?)*
